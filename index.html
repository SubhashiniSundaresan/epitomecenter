Rehabilitation Psychology

Healing Hearts Healing Lives

Every day within Epitome Center, we deliver individualized therapy to help each child reach their fullest potential in school, at home, and in their community. While every child’s journey at Epitome Center is unique, our commitment to providing a welcoming and nurturing environment never wavers. We invite you to get a glimpse into a typical day at a Epitome Center, and the impact it can have on your child’s future. Because at Epitome Center, little wins can make a BIG difference.

Get Started Today

Get In Touch

Our family support specialists will get in touch. During our conversation with you, we will schedule an introductory meeting for your family.

Introductory Meeting and Center Tour

Scheduled within a week, this is your opportunity to meet the specialists and learn more about our program.

Clinical Assessment

Before the start of therapy, we will provide a clinical assessment, including an individualized treatment plan that we will review with you.

Therapy Begins!

A highly trained behavior technician will work with your child using the treatment plan developed by our clinical team to meet the child’s needs and goals.

Parent training

Since parents are the most important member of any child’s team, we will teach you the strategies we are using with your child to support your child’s development.

Review and analysis

The data and information that is collected during your child's therapy sessions is used to continually assess your child’s progress and tailor the therapy accordingly.
